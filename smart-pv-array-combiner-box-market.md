<p><p>The Smart PV Array Combiner Box market is divided into two main types: DC Smart PV Combiner Boxes and AC Smart PV Combiner Boxes. DC Smart PV Combiner Boxes aggregate multiple solar panel outputs at the direct current level, enhancing efficiency and simplifying installations. In contrast, AC Smart PV Combiner Boxes manage outputs from inverters, allowing for advanced monitoring and control at the alternating current stage. Both types optimize energy flow and improve overall system performance in solar power installations.</p></p>
