<p><p>Rotary dryers are essential in various industries for moisture removal from materials. The market comprises two main types: directly heated and indirectly heated rotary dryers. Directly heated dryers use a combustion source to heat air that directly contacts the material, resulting in efficient drying but potential contamination. In contrast, indirectly heated dryers utilize heated walls or surfaces, preventing direct contact with combustion gases, thus ensuring product purity. Both types cater to different applications based on drying requirements and material sensitivity.</p></p>
