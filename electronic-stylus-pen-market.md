<p><p>The electronic stylus pen market consists of several types, primarily categorized into electromagnetic pressure-sensitive stylus pens, capacitive touch stylus pens, and others. Electromagnetic pressure-sensitive stylus pens utilize advanced technology to detect pressure levels for precise drawing and writing on compatible screens. Capacitive touch stylus pens, on the other hand, interact with capacitive screens through electrical conductivity, making them widely compatible with various devices. The "others" category includes niche stylus types, such as those designed for specific applications or enhanced functionality.</p></p>
