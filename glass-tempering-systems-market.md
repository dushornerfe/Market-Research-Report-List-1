<p><p>Glass tempering systems are categorized into various types based on their configurations and operational methodologies. Horizontal systems are used for flat glass tempering, while vertical systems are designed for larger pieces. Continuous systems ensure uninterrupted production, whereas oscillating systems move glass back and forth for uniform heating. Bent systems cater to curved glass applications. Uni-directional systems process glass in one direction, while bi-directional systems allow for dual-direction handling, enhancing versatility and efficiency in production processes.</p></p>
