<p><p>The polyolefins market utilizes various catalysts to enhance polymer production. Normal metallocene catalysts are single-site catalysts that enable controlled polymerization, leading to uniform and high-performance polyolefins. In contrast, dinuclear metallocene catalysts consist of two metal centers, offering improved catalyst activity and versatility in producing copolymers with tailored properties. Both types facilitate the production of advanced materials, catering to diverse applications in packaging, automotive, and construction industries while improving efficiency and sustainability in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
