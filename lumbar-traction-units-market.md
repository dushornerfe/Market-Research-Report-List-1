<p><p>Lumbar traction units are categorized into two main types: electronic and normal (manual) traction units. Electronic traction units utilize motorized components to deliver controlled and consistent traction, allowing for precise adjustments in force and duration, enhancing patient comfort and treatment efficacy. In contrast, normal traction units rely on manual settings or gravitational forces, requiring healthcare providers to adjust the parameters manually. Both types aim to relieve lumbar spine pain by decompressing vertebral joints and improving spinal alignment.</p></p>
