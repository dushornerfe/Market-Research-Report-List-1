<p><p>The advanced lead-acid battery market comprises several types, including Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid (VRLA) batteries, flooded batteries, and others. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, making them suitable for applications where leak prevention is critical. Flooded batteries have a liquid electrolyte, offering higher durability and capacity for heavy-duty applications but requiring regular maintenance. Other variations may include gel and absorbed glass mat (AGM) batteries, catering to diverse sectors like renewable energy, automotive, and industrial backup systems.</p></p>
