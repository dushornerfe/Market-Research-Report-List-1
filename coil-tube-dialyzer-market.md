<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coil tube dialyzer market comprises two primary types based on membrane characteristics: low flux and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes permit a slower rate of solute transfer, making them suitable for patients requiring gentle dialysis. In contrast, high flux membranes allow for a faster and more efficient removal of larger molecules and waste products, catering to patients with higher dialysis needs. Both types serve distinct patient requirements in the field of renal therapy, enhancing treatment efficacy and outcomes.</p></p>
