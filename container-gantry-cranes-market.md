<p><p>In the Container Gantry Cranes market, there are two primary types: Single Main Girder and Double Main Girder cranes. Single Main Girder cranes feature a single beam design, making them suitable for lighter loads and limited space, typically in smaller operations. In contrast, Double Main Girder cranes have two beams, providing enhanced stability and load capacity, which makes them ideal for larger ports and heavy-duty applications. Both types serve vital roles in container handling and logistics efficiency.</p></p>
