<p><strong>The Photocured Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free-radical Type Photoinitiator</li><li>Cationic Type Photoinitiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photocured materials market primarily consists of two types of photoinitiators: free-radical and cationic. Free-radical photoinitiators generate reactive radicals upon exposure to light, initiating polymerization in applications like coatings and adhesives. In contrast, cationic photoinitiators produce cationic species, leading to polymerization through ionic mechanisms, which are often used in more specialized applications like electronics and dental materials. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and efficiency of photocurable products across various industries.</p></p>
