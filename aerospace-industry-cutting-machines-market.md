<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes various cutting machines based on specific needs. Disc-type machines employ circular saw blades for precise cuts, ideal for metals and composite materials. Water jet types utilize high-pressure water streams to cut through various materials without thermal distortion. Laser type machines use focused light beams for high precision and clean cuts, suitable for intricate designs. Knife-type machines use sharp blades for soft materials, while "others" encompass emerging technologies or hybrid systems, catering to diverse aerospace applications.</p></p>
