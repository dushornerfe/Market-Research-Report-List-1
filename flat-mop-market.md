<p><p>The flat mop market is divided into two main types: single side and double side. Single side flat mops feature a cleaning surface on one side, making them straightforward and ideal for quick tasks. In contrast, double side flat mops incorporate cleaning surfaces on both sides, allowing users to effectively clean larger areas without frequent changing or washing of the mop pad. The choice between these types depends on user preference, cleaning efficiency, and the specific cleaning environment.</p></p>
