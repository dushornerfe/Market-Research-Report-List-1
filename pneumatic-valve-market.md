<p><p>The pneumatic valve market can be categorized into two main types: switching type and regulated type valves. Switching type valves are designed to control the flow of air or gas by opening and closing an airway, typically used in applications requiring quick on/off operation. In contrast, regulated type valves maintain a consistent pressure within a system, providing precise control over airflow and pressure output. Both types play crucial roles in automation, enhancing efficiency and performance in various industrial processes.</p></p>
