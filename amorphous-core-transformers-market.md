<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed Amorphous Core Transformers</li><li>Dry-Type Amorphous Core Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amorphous core transformer market comprises two primary types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed amorphous core transformers utilize oil for cooling and insulation, offering high efficiency and reduced energy losses, making them suitable for utility applications. In contrast, dry-type amorphous core transformers use air for cooling and insulation, making them safer and more environmentally friendly, ideal for indoor installations and areas with fire hazards. Both types contribute to enhanced energy efficiency and reduced environmental impact in electrical systems.</p></p>
