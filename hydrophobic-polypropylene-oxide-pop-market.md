<p><strong>The Hydrophobic Polypropylene Oxide (POP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Mixing Type</li><li>Chemical Graft Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hydrophobic Polypropylene Oxide (POP) market consists primarily of two types: mechanical mixing type and chemical graft type. The mechanical mixing type involves physically blending polypropylene oxide with other materials to enhance hydrophobicity without altering the chemical structure. In contrast, the chemical graft type creates a covalent bond between polypropylene oxide and other polymers, resulting in tailored hydrophobic properties and improved functionality. These types cater to various applications, including coatings, adhesives, and specialty materials, meeting specific performance needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
