<p><strong>The Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Graft Copolymerization</li><li>Bulk Copolymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) resin market comprises two primary types: emulsion graft copolymerization and bulk copolymerization. Emulsion graft copolymerization involves the polymerization of monomers in an aqueous emulsion, promoting improved impact resistance and surface finish. In contrast, bulk copolymerization occurs in a solid phase, resulting in high molecular weight and superior mechanical properties. Both methods are essential for producing ABS resins that meet diverse industrial applications, ranging from automotive to consumer goods, by tailoring physical characteristics.</p></p>
