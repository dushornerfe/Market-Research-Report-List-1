<p><ul><li>Surface Resistivity Meter</li><li>Volume Resistivity Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital resistivity meters are essential tools used to measure the electrical resistivity of materials, with key types including surface and volume resistivity meters. Surface resistivity meters assess the resistivity of a material's surface, making them ideal for evaluating coatings and concrete. In contrast, volume resistivity meters measure the resistivity throughout a material's entire volume, suitable for testing bulk substances like soils and rocks. These meters find applications in various fields, including construction, geophysics, and materials science.</p></p>
