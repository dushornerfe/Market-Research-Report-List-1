<p><p>The garbage bag market includes various materials, each serving different needs. Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and durable, ideal for general waste. Liner low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) offers enhanced strength and puncture resistance, suitable for heavy items. High-density polyethylene (HDPE) is more rigid and is often used for sharp trash. Polypropylene (PP) is lightweight and recyclable, catering to eco-conscious consumers. Other types may include biodegradable and compostable options, addressing environmental concerns and diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
