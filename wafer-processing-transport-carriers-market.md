<p><p>Wafer processing transport carriers are essential for the semiconductor industry, facilitating the safe movement of wafers during manufacturing. Front Opening Unified Pods (FOUPs) are widely used due to their design that minimizes contamination and enables efficient handling in automated environments. SMIF pods also provide a clean, controlled environment for wafers but have a different approach to loading and unloading. Other types include cassettes and carriers tailored for specific manufacturing processes, each contributing to the overall efficiency and safety of wafer transport.</p></p>
