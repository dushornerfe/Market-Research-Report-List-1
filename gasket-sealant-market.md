<p><p>The gasket sealant market comprises various types, notably liquid sealants and anaerobic sealants. Liquid sealants are versatile, easy to apply, and cure upon exposure to air, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. In contrast, anaerobic sealants are designed to cure in the absence of air, providing strong bonding and sealing in metal-to-metal applications, commonly used in automotive and industrial sectors. Both types cater to distinct sealing needs, enhancing durability and reliability in various environments.</p></p>
