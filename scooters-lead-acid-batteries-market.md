<p><p>The scooters lead-acid batteries market primarily features two types: Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries, which include absorbent glass mat (AGM) and gel types, are sealed, maintenance-free, and suitable for various scooter applications. They offer efficient power delivery and safety benefits. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance, including water addition, but provide robust performance and are often used in budget-conscious settings. Both types cater to specific needs in the scooter market.</p></p>
