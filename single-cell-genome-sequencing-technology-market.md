<p><p>The Single-Cell Genome Sequencing Technology market includes various methods such as Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS), Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), quantitative PCR (qPCR), Microarrays, and Multiple Displacement Amplification (MDA). NGS provides high-throughput sequencing, allowing deep insights into individual cell genetics. PCR and qPCR enable specific DNA amplification, enhancing sensitivity for low-input samples. Microarrays analyze gene expression across multiple genes simultaneously, while MDA amplifies the entire genome from single cells, crucial for comprehensive genomic analysis. Each method serves unique applications in research and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
