<p><p>The bubble tea chain market can be categorized into two types based on preparation methods: hand-cranking and electric-cranking. Hand-cranking involves manual preparation, offering a traditional, artisanal experience that appeals to customers seeking authenticity. This method often emphasizes craftsmanship and can create unique flavor profiles. In contrast, electric-cranking utilizes machines for faster and more consistent production, catering to high-demand environments. This method enhances efficiency and scalability, making it suitable for larger bubble tea chains aiming for quick service and wide distribution.</p></p>
