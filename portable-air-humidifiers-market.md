<p><p>The portable air humidifiers market includes two main types: warm mist and cool mist humidifiers. Warm mist humidifiers heat water to produce steam, which adds warmth and moisture to the air, ideal for colder climates. In contrast, cool mist humidifiers disperse a fine mist of cool water, helping to cool and humidify spaces, making them suitable for warmer environments. Both types effectively relieve dryness and enhance comfort, catering to various user preferences and environmental conditions.</p></p>
