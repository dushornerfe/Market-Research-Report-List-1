Manual machines require user skill to control every aspect of the brewing process, offering complete customization. Semi-automatic machines automate water flow but still allow users to manage grind size and extraction time. Fully automatic machines simplify the process further by automating both water flow and dosage, appealing to those seeking convenience without sacrificing quality. Each type caters to different preferences and expertise levels.</p></p>
