<p><p>The waterless urinals market features several product types designed to minimize water usage. Membrane traps utilize a flexible membrane that seals in waste and prevents odors without water. Biological blocks release enzymes to break down waste naturally, further limiting smell. Mechanical balls operate with floating components that create an effective seal and assist with waste flow, enhancing hygiene without water consumption.</p></p>
