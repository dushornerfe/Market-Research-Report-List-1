<p><p>MRI Pulse Oximeters are specialized medical devices designed to measure the oxygen saturation levels in patients undergoing magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) procedures. These advanced pulse oximeters utilize non-magnetic components and innovative technologies, ensuring reliable readings during MRI scans without compromising patient safety.</p><p>The MRI Pulse Oximeters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.5% during the forecast period. Key trends include the integration of wireless technology for remote monitoring capabilities and the development of compact, user-friendly devices.
