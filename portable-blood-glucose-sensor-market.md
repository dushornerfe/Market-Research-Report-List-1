<p><p>The Portable Blood Glucose Sensor market consists of two main types: minimally invasive and non-invasive sensors. Minimally invasive sensors require a small puncture, like finger pricking, to obtain a blood sample for glucose testing, providing accurate results. In contrast, non-invasive sensors measure glucose levels without breaking the skin, using technologies like optical or electromagnetic sensors. Both types cater to the growing demand for convenient diabetes management, enhancing patient comfort and compliance while monitoring blood sugar levels on-the-go.</p></p>
