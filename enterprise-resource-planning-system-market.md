<p><p>Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems are categorized mainly into two market types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. On-premise ERP is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ERP offers a subscription model hosted on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and lower initial costs. This flexibility makes cloud-based solutions increasingly popular among businesses seeking agility and reduced IT overhead.</p></p>
