<p><ul><li>Synthetic Gear Oil</li><li>Mineral Gear Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind turbine gear oil market primarily consists of synthetic and mineral gear oils. Synthetic gear oils are crafted for high performance, offering superior thermal stability, oxidation resistance, and lower friction properties, which enhance turbine efficiency and longevity. In contrast, mineral gear oils are derived from refined crude oil and provide basic lubrication and protection at a lower cost, though they may not perform as well under extreme conditions. Both types are essential for maintaining wind turbine functionality and reliability.</p></p>
