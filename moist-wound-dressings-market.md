<p><p>The Moist Wound Dressings market includes various types designed to enhance healing by maintaining a moist environment. Foam dressings provide cushioning and absorb exudate, while hydrocolloids form a gel to keep wounds moist and protect from bacteria. Alginates, derived from seaweed, are highly absorbent and promote fluid management. Antimicrobials actively prevent infection, transparent films allow for easy monitoring, and hydrogels provide hydration to dry wounds. Together, these types cater to diverse wound care needs, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
