<p><ul><li>Rigid Harvesting Header</li><li>Flex Harvesting Header</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Harvesting headers are essential attachments used in combine harvesters, tailored for different crop types and field conditions. The rigid harvesting header features a fixed design ideal for straight, even fields, providing stability and efficient cutting of crops like wheat and barley. In contrast, the flex harvesting header has a flexible cutting platform that adapts to uneven terrain, allowing for better performance on diverse landscapes and optimizing the harvest of low-lying or irregularly shaped crops, such as soybeans.</p></p>
