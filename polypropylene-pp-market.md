<p><strong>The Polypropylene (PP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homopolymer</li><li>Copolymer</li><li>Impact copolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) market is segmented into three main types: homopolymer, copolymer, and impact copolymer. Homopolymer polypropylene is characterized by its high strength and rigidity, making it suitable for a variety of applications. Copolymer polypropylene, produced by polymerizing propylene with other monomers, offers enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for automotive and packaging uses. Impact copolymer polypropylene combines the properties of both homopolymer and copolymer, providing superior toughness, making it suitable for durable applications like containers and automotive parts.</p></p>
