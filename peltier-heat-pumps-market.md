<p><ul><li>Multi-Stage</li><li>Single-Stage</li><li>Thermocyclers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peltier heat pumps market is categorized into three main types: multi-stage, single-stage, and thermocyclers. Single-stage Peltier devices utilize a single thermoelectric module for heating or cooling, suitable for compact applications. Multi-stage systems consist of multiple modules stacked for enhanced temperature differentials, often used in industrial settings. Thermocyclers are specialized for rapid temperature changes, crucial in laboratory environments for processes like PCR. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing efficiency, size, and thermal management needs.</p></p>
