<p><ul><li>Ablative</li><li>Non-Ablative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cosmetic laser market is primarily divided into two main types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layers of skin, promoting significant skin rejuvenation, tightening, and texture improvement, often used for deeper scars and wrinkles. Non-ablative lasers, in contrast, work by penetrating the skin without removing layers, stimulating collagen production and addressing issues like pigmentation and fine lines with minimal downtime. Both types cater to diverse aesthetic needs, influencing market growth and consumer preferences.</p></p>
