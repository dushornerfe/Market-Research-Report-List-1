<p><p>The 3D IC and 2.5D IC packaging market consists of three primary types: 3D TSV (Through-Silicon Via), which facilitates vertical stacking of chips for higher performance and reduced footprint; 2.5D packaging, which uses an interposer to connect multiple chips laterally, enabling improved power efficiency and communication; and 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP), where chips are packaged at the wafer level for compact size and enhanced thermal performance. Each type offers distinct advantages in miniaturization and functionality.</p></p>
