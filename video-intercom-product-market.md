<p><p>The video intercom market is primarily divided into two types: Analog and IP. Analog video intercoms use traditional wiring and transmit video and audio signals over coaxial or twisted pair cables, offering basic functionality and lower initial costs but limited integration capabilities. In contrast, IP video intercoms leverage internet protocol technology, allowing for high-definition video, remote access, and integration with smart devices and other security systems. This type offers enhanced features and scalability, catering to modern security needs.</p></p>
