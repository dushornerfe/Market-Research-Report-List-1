<p><p>Gelcoating is a protective and aesthetic layer applied to composite materials, commonly used in industries such as marine, automotive, and construction. Advances in technology have led to the development of faster-curing gelcoats and those with improved adhesion properties. Furthermore, the expansion of the construction industry, particularly in developing regions, is boosting demand for gelcoats in architectural elements and infrastructure projects. Overall, the gelcoating market is evolving with innovations and increasing applications across diverse sectors, positioning it for sustained growth in the coming years.</p></p>
