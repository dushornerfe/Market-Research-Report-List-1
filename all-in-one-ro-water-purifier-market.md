<p><ul><li>POU All-In-One RO Water Purifier</li><li>POE All-In-One RO Water Purifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The All-In-One RO Water Purifier market is categorized into two main types: Point of Use (POU) and Point of Entry (POE) systems. POU All-In-One RO Water Purifiers are designed for installation at a single tap, providing filtered water directly where it's needed, ideal for residential use. In contrast, POE All-In-One RO Water Purifiers treat water at the entry point of a home or building, ensuring clean water supply throughout the entire property, making it suitable for larger households or commercial applications.</p></p>
