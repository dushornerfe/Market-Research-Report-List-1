<p><ul><li>Volumetric Pipette</li><li>Graduated Pipette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The measuring pipette market comprises two main types: volumetric pipettes and graduated pipettes. Volumetric pipettes are designed for precise measurement of a single volume, offering high accuracy for laboratory applications. In contrast, graduated pipettes feature a scale along their length, allowing for measurement of varying volumes, though with slightly less precision. Both types serve essential roles in scientific research, quality control, and clinical diagnostics, catering to the diverse needs of laboratories and institutions seeking reliable liquid measurement tools.</p></p>
