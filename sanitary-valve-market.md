<p><p>The sanitary valve market includes several key types, each serving specific applications in the food, beverage, and pharmaceutical industries. Ball valves offer tight sealing and quick operation, ideal for on/off control. Butterfly valves provide flow regulation with a compact design, suitable for large systems. Diaphragm valves feature a flexible diaphragm that isolates the flow, ensuring sanitary conditions. Other valves, such as check and pinch valves, fulfill niche requirements, enhancing system efficacy while maintaining hygiene standards in critical processes.</p></p>
