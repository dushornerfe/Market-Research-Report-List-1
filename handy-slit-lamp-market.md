<p><p>The Handy Slit Lamp market is divided mainly into two types: Monocular and Binocular Slit Lamps. Monocular Slit Lamps feature a single eyepiece, offering a simplified, lightweight design ideal for portability and basic examinations. In contrast, Binocular Slit Lamps utilize dual eyepieces, providing enhanced depth perception and a more comprehensive view, making them suitable for detailed diagnostics and advanced procedures. Both types cater to different user needs, from casual screening to specialized clinical applications.</p></p>
