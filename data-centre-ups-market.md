<p><strong>The Data Centre UPS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Battery-Operated</li><li>Flywheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Centre UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) market features two primary types: battery-operated systems and flywheel systems. Battery-operated UPS units are commonly used for short-term power backup, employing batteries to store energy for immediate use during outages. Conversely, flywheel UPS systems utilize kinetic energy stored in a rotating mass, providing instantaneous backup and longevity with minimal maintenance. Both types serve critical functions in ensuring data centres maintain continuous operations and protect sensitive equipment from power disruptions.</p></p>
